Atheneum Learning begins and ends with helping clinicians achieve greater outcomes with their patients. This can only be accomplished if the clinician can provide treatment that is known to be effective.

Unfortunately, academic research does not translate quickly to real-world practice. Because the clinicians have trouble translating the research into treatment. Until Atheneum Learning.

Atheneum Learning employs innovative teaching methods to train professionals and students in the most current evidence based treatment approaches to mental health via highly interactive multimedia programs, published information, and workshops to ensure that their patients receive the care they deserve.

Atheneum Learning's training programs enable professionals to improve their clinical decision making - giving them the tools to think in contingencies - and ultimately provide the most effective treatment possible to their patients. Our easy-to-use, programs ensure that the learning process is as productive, efficient, and intuitive as possible. With training modules fit specifically to your needs, Atheneum Learning is the most relevant and effective choice for interactive, psychology, learning programs.

About Edmund C. Neuhaus, PhD, ABPP

Edmund C. Neuhaus, founded Atheneum Learning to teach the most current and effective evidence-based treatment approaches in behavioral health. The breakthrough TRi Training System couples the content of these approaches with a best-in-class e-learning instructional technology to maximize learning effectiveness and ultimately produce better patient outcomes.
